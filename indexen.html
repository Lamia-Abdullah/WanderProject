  • Winter at Tantora

    Alula

    An annual event in Al-Ula governorate, where the people celebrate and exchange congratulations for their return to the fields, the return of the planting season, and the entry of winter. Tantora is named after the sundial, which is located in the southeastern part of the village, as the people of the region depended on it to know the time of the beginning of the planting season and the change of seasons. The festival includes recreational, cultural and sports activities and provides field visits to archaeological sites in the region. The events included 13 archaeological and cultural sites, in addition to musical theatrical performances, including the Maraya Theater in the city.
